📋 SASSA Grant Application Assistance

We help older persons, people with disabilities, and caregivers apply for social grants.

Get Quote →

📌 What We Do

  • Check eligibility for Older Person's Grant, Disability Grant, or Child Support Grant
  • Complete all SASSA application forms
  • Gather required supporting documents (ID, proof of income, medical reports)
  • Submit application at the local SASSA office (or guide you through the process)
  • Follow up on application status

💰 Pricing

R 300 (fixed fee – excludes any medical assessment fees)

Turnaround: 2‑3 days for preparation; SASSA processing 1‑3 months.

You pay SASSA directly for any required assessments.

📄 What You Need to Provide

  • Certified copy of ID
  • Proof of residence
  • Proof of income (payslips, affidavit if unemployed)
  • Medical report / disability certificate (for Disability Grant)
  • Birth certificates of children (for Child Support Grant)
