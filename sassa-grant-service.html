📌 What we do

  • Complete all SASSA application forms

  • Submit application at the local SASSA office (or guide you through the process)
  • Follow up on application status

📄 What you need to provide

  • Certified copy of ID
  • Proof of residence
  • Proof of income (payslips, affidavit if unemployed)
  • Medical report / disability certificate (for Disability Grant)
  • Birth certificates of children (for Child Support Grant)
