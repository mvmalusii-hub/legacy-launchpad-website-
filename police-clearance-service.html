🛂 Police Clearance Certificate Assistance

We guide you through the process of obtaining your South African Police Clearance Certificate – fast, easy, and no queues.

Get Quote →

📌 What We Do

  • Check your eligibility and requirements
  • Complete the SAPS application form (DHA-91 / SAPS 91)
  • Guide you on fingerprint submission (SAPS 91(a))
  • Prepare the supporting documents checklist
  • Assist with submission at SAPS Criminal Record Centre
  • Follow up on the status of your application

💰 Pricing

R 350 (fixed fee – excludes SAPS fees paid by you)

Turnaround: 2‑3 days for preparation; SAPS processing 4‑8 weeks.

You pay the SAPS fee directly (currently ~R 150 – R 200, subject to change).

📄 What You Need to Provide

  • Certified copy of your ID (or passport for non‑citizens)
  • Proof of residential address
  • Completed SAPS fingerprint form (we provide guidance)
  • Additional documents required for name changes (marriage, divorce, etc.)

Important Information

  • Police Clearance Certificates are required for employment, visas, and professional registrations.
  • Processing time varies – we'll help you track your application.
  • Fingerprints must be taken at an SAPS police station – we'll guide you on the process.
