📋 Document Roadmap

Phase 1: Registration & Compliance (9 docs)

✔ Document Description
IT-001 – CIPC Registration Certificate Proof of company registration
IT-002 – Memorandum of Incorporation (MOI) Company constitution
IT-003 – SARS Registration (IT, PAYE, VAT) Tax compliance (VAT if turnover >R1m)
IT-004 – COIDA Registration Employee injury cover
IT-005 – Public Liability Insurance Cover for client site visits
IT-006 – Professional Indemnity Insurance For software errors, data loss – critical
IT-007 – Cyber Liability Insurance For data breaches, ransomware
IT-008 – B‑BBEE Certificate For corporate and government tenders
IT-009 – CSD Registration For government tenders

✔ Document Description

IT-013 – Non‑Disclosure Agreement (NDA) Protect source code, client data
IT-014 – Data Processing Agreement (DPA) For POPIA compliance (client data)
IT-015 – Hosting / Server Colocation Agreement With data centre provider
IT-016 – Software Licence Agreement Terms of use for selling software
IT-017 – Quotation / Proposal Template Per project
