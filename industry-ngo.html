✔DocumentPurpose

NPO‑003 – SARS Registration (IT, PAYE, VAT)Tax compliance (VAT if turnover > R1m)
NPO‑004 – COIDA RegistrationEmployee injury cover
NPO‑005 – Public Liability InsuranceCover for events, visitors, beneficiaries
NPO‑006 – NPO Registration Certificate (DSD)Required for fundraising, grants
NPO‑007 – PBO Registration Certificate (SARS)For tax-deductible donations (Section 18A)
NPO‑008 – Section 18A Certificate (if applicable)For issuing donation receipts
NPO‑009 – B‑BBEE Certificate (or affidavit)For corporate funding
NPO‑010 – CSD RegistrationFor government tenders

NPO‑012 – CIPC Annual Return (if registered as NPC)Compliance

NPO‑014 – Child Protection Clearance (if working with children)Police clearance for staff/volunteers
