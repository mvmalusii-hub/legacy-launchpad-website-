📌 Services We Offer for Individuals

UIF Applications

Unemployment Insurance Fund (UIF) claims – we help you complete and submit the required forms, track your application, and follow up with the Department of Labour.

ID & Passport Applications

Assistance with South African ID applications, re‑issues, and passport renewals. We guide you through the e‑HomeAffairs process and help you gather the correct documents.

Professional Artist Profiles

One‑page artist bios, CVs, portfolios, and EPK (electronic press kits). Perfect for musicians, visual artists, actors, and creatives applying for grants or gigs.

Police Clearance Certificates

We guide you through the process, help complete the application, and arrange fingerprint submission – so you don't have to stand in long queues.

Personal CV & Cover Letter

Professional, ATS‑friendly CVs and custom cover letters for job applications. We also help with LinkedIn profile optimisation.

Social Grant Applications

Assistance with SASSA older person’s grant, disability grant, and child support grant applications – including document gathering and submission.
