📄 UIF Application Assistance

We help you complete and submit your Unemployment Insurance Fund (UIF) claim – fast, affordable, and no queues.

Get Quote →

📌 What we do

We assist individuals who have lost their jobs or been temporarily laid off to claim UIF benefits. Our service includes:

  • Checking your eligibility
  • Completing all required UIF forms (UI-2.1, UI-2.2, etc.)
  • Gathering supporting documents (ID, payslips, termination letter)
  • Submitting your claim online or at the labour centre
  • Following up on your application status

💰 Pricing

R 350 (fixed fee – excludes any government charges)

Turnaround: 3‑5 days for preparation; Department of Labour processing takes 4‑8 weeks.

📄 What you need to provide

  • Certified copy of your ID
  • Last 6 months' payslips (if available)
  • Letter of termination / dismissal / retrenchment
  • Bank account details (for payment)
