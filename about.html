About Legacy Launchpad

Legacy Launchpad (Pty) Ltd was founded by Msimelelo Victor Malusi – a compliance and documentation specialist who has built systems for NPOs, security companies, and small businesses from the ground up. The name Msimelelo means “a walking stick / a pillar of support” – and that is exactly what this consultancy offers: reliable, steady support for entrepreneurs who need professional administrative systems without the high cost of lawyers or accountants.

We believe that every business owner, no matter how small, deserves access to clear, organised, and compliant documentation. Whether you are registering a company, applying for a licence, or setting up an NPO, the paperwork should not stand in your way. Legacy Launchpad exists to remove that friction – giving you a ready‑to‑use system of document roadmaps, file directory structures, policies, and forms, all tailored to your specific industry.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower entrepreneurs, small businesses, and non‑profit organisations with affordable, professional, and practical compliance and documentation systems – enabling them to focus on their core mission while we handle the administrative backbone.

🌟 Our Vision

A South Africa where every startup and community organisation has access to structured, audit‑ready documentation – reducing regulatory failures, protecting legacies, and building a culture of compliance from the ground up.

💡 Our Core Values

Integrity

We do what we say. No hidden fees, no false promises.

Precision

Every document, every folder, every detail matters.

Support

Legacy

We help you build what lasts – a business that endures.

Unlike traditional consultancies that charge by the hour, Legacy Launchpad works on fixed, transparent fees. You know the cost before we start. Our products – from File Directory Structures to Document Roadmaps to full policy drafting – are designed to be immediately usable. You don't need to be a legal expert or an IT administrator. Just follow the roadmap, copy the folder structure, and you are audit‑ready.

We are not lawyers, accountants, or tax practitioners. We do not give legal advice or represent you in court. What we do is provide the administrative scaffolding that every compliant business needs – the policies, forms, registers, and file organisation that keep you out of trouble and ready for inspections.

Whether you are a construction company needing CIDB compliance, a daycare applying for DSD registration, a scrap yard managing daily purchase registers, or an artist building your EPK – Legacy Launchpad is your partner in building a legacy that lasts.
