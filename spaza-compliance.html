Compliance Drive – City of Ekurhuleni

All spaza shops and food-handling businesses must be fully compliant with municipal by-laws and the Business Act. Missing documents or health violations can lead to closure, fines, or legal action.

I help you navigate the process – from document gap analysis to final submission and follow-up.

Mandatory Requirements

  • Completed business license application
  • Zoning certificate / written consent
  • Certificate of Acceptability (health)
  • Approved building plan + occupancy cert
  • Title deed / landlord letter
  • Valid ID or work permit
  • CIPC registration certificate
  • SARS tax clearance certificate
Missing any document = application rejected. I solve this.
