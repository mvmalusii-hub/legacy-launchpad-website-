📌 What We Offer

CIPC Company Registration

Pty Ltd, NPC, Sole Proprietor registration. We handle name reservation, MOI, and all CIPC forms. You get your registration documents within 5‑10 working days.

SARS Tax Registration

Income Tax, PAYE, and VAT registration. We guide you through eFiling and submit the necessary forms.

B‑BBEE Affidavit

For Exempted Micro Enterprises (EME) with turnover under R10m. Get your sworn affidavit within 1 day – required for many tenders and corporate clients.

COIDA Registration

Workmen's compensation registration – mandatory for any business with employees. We help you register and obtain your letter of good standing.

CIPC Annual Returns

We file your annual return (CoR 25.1) and pay the prescribed fee – on time, every time. Avoid penalties.

Liquor Licence Assistance

Documentation, form completion, site plan and photo guidance for off‑consumption licences (bottle stores). We don't represent you at the board, but we get your application pack ready.

UIF & PAYE Registration

Register your employees for UIF and PAYE with SARS. We also assist with monthly declarations (separate fee).

Industry Document Roadmaps

Full document lists and file directory structures for 20+ industries – from construction to NPOs. Perfect for starting any business.
